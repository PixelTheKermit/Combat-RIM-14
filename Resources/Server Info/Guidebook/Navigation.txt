#Debris Indication
Your navigational mass scanner will automatically color recognized objects based on simple classification.

- [color=#FFFFFF]Strongly metallic debris[/color] is shown as white.
- [color=#B2CFCF]High water content rocky debris[/color] is shown as blue-gray.
- [color=#F4A460]Low water content rocky debris[/color] is shown as sandy brown.
- [color=#7FFFD4]Vessels with IFF[/color] are shown as aquamarine.
- [color=#FF00FF]Atypical structures[/color] are shown in magenta.
